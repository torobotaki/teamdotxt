* Tabs define groupings of sub-tasks
* Parentheses () are name(s) of person(s) responsible
* Square brackets [] are deadlines
* A plus sign followed by a number is the number of days necessary to complete task, example +2
* Curly brackets {} are used to indicate status: {In Progress}, {Done}
* Numbering listed elements (sub-tasks) gives dependencies. Ex. number 2 will only take place when number 1 is completed
